Integration of Personnel Tracking in an Augmented Reality Environment

What is the project?
This past semester I started on my senior design project. The goal of the project is to build a system that can track employees in an indoor or outdoor setting. Then, with the hololens, display others in real time in an augmented reality environment.
What is my role?
My role in the team is of the Software Architect. I help the team with choosing the best way to connect our different services and systems to create a platform that is scalable as well as easy to integrate with. I also take on DevOps roles so that the project has a solid CI/CD pipeline to deliver the fastest and best product to our customer.
